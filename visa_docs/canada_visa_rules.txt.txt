=== CANADA VISITOR VISA RULES ===
Source: Government of Canada - IRCC

WHO NEEDS A VISA:
- Citizens of most countries need a visitor visa (temporary resident visa) to enter Canada
- US citizens do not need a visa but need an eTA
- Citizens of visa-exempt countries need an eTA instead of a visa

TYPES OF VISITOR VISAS:
- Single-entry visa: Enter Canada once. If you leave, you need a new visa (except travel to US or St. Pierre and Miquelon)
- Multiple-entry visa: Enter and exit Canada multiple times while visa is valid

REQUIREMENTS:
- Valid passport
- Sufficient funds for your stay
- No criminal record
- Medical exam may be required
- Leave Canada at the end of your authorized stay

HOW TO APPLY:
- Fill out IMM 5257 form
- Pay fee
- Provide biometrics (fingerprints and photo)
- Wait for processing

PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES:
- Cannot work in Canada without a work permit
- Cannot study without a study permit (unless short course under 6 months)

DURATION OF STAY:
- Usually 6 months from date of entry
- Officer may authorize shorter or longer stay
