=== UK VISITOR VISA RULES ===
Source: UK Government - GOV.UK

STANDARD VISITOR VISA:
- Allows stay up to 6 months
- Purpose: Tourism, visiting family, business activities, short study

PERMITTED BUSINESS ACTIVITIES:
- Attend meetings, conferences, seminars
- Negotiate and sign deals
- Give lectures (if invited by higher education institution)
- Legal proceedings as advocate or witness

PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES:
- Paid or unpaid work for UK company
- Self-employment in UK
- Work placement or internship
- Selling goods directly to public

ELECTRONIC TRAVEL AUTHORISATION (ETA):
- Required for some nationalities
- Valid for up to 6 months
- Can be used for permitted business and academic activities

VISA TYPES UNDER APPENDIX V:
1. Standard Visitor (6 months, can apply for 2/5/10 years)
2. Marriage/Civil Partnership Visitor
3. Transit Visitor (up to 48 hours)
4. Diplomatic Visa Arrangement Visitor

REQUIREMENTS:
- Genuine visitor (will leave UK at end of stay)
- Sufficient funds
- No intention to live in UK through frequent visits
